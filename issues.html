Fix Your $1 Website Builder Issues

Building a $1 website with Grok AI, IONOS, and Netlify is exciting, but issues like DNS errors or missing emails can arise. Here’s how to troubleshoot and keep your site running smoothly!

We faced hiccups building $1 Web Revolution. Below are common issues, fixes, and tips to use Grok AI for debugging your $1 website builder.

1. IONOS Account Mix-Ups

Multiple IONOS accounts caused our domain to vanish from the dashboard.

Fix: Use one email at IONOS. If your domain is missing, contact support (1-844-494-9067 or support@ionos.com) with your Customer ID and Contract Number.

Pro Tip: Ask Grok to draft a support email (e.g., “Write an email to IONOS about my missing domain”).

2. Missing ICANN Verification Email

No ICANN email means your domain won’t activate.

Fix: Check spam/junk. In IONOS > Domains > Privacy & Contact Details, update email and resend. Contact support if needed.

Pro Tip: Set email filters for IONOS. Ask Grok to review your setup (e.g., “Did I miss a step in IONOS?”).

3. DNS Misconfiguration

Wrong DNS settings cause “server not found” errors.

Fix: In IONOS > Domains > DNS, add:

  • CNAME: Host = www, Value = webfor1.netlify.app, TTL = 3600.
  • A record: Host = @, Value = 75.2.60.5, TTL = 3600.

Delete conflicting A/AAAA records. Wait 1-2 hours, check at dnschecker.org.

Pro Tip: Ask Grok to verify DNS (e.g., “Check my CNAME for www.webfor1.com”). Test in a private tab.

4. Site Display Issues

Images or layouts may break due to coding errors or missing files.

Fix: Verify files (index.html, /images/hero.jpg) in GitHub root or images folder. Check Netlify’s Deploys tab. Ask Grok to fix (e.g., “My image is cropped—help!”).

Pro Tip: Clear cache or test in a private tab. Ask Grok for style tweaks (e.g., “Make images full-width”).

5. Images Not Loading

Incorrect paths or large files can break images.

Fix: Check paths (e.g., /images/issues.jpg) in HTML. Ensure images are in GitHub’s images folder. Use JPGs under 100 KiB at tinypng.com. Test in a private tab.

Pro Tip: Ask Grok to check paths (e.g., “Why isn’t my issues.jpg loading?”).

6. Mobile Responsiveness Issues

Your site may look off on mobile due to missing responsive settings.

Fix: Ensure is in HTML. Use Tailwind’s responsive classes (e.g., md:text-xl). Ask Grok to adjust CSS (e.g., “Fix my navbar for mobile”). Test on iPhone/Safari.

Pro Tip: Use Safari’s Responsive Design Mode or ask Grok to optimize (e.g., “Make my site mobile-friendly”).

Fixed your issue? Head to our Guide to keep building, or ask Grok for custom fixes!
